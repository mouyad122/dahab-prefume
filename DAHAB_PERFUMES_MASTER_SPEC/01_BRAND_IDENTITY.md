# 01. BRAND IDENTITY

## 1. Brand Name Rules
The brand is officially registered as **DAHAB PERFUMES**. 

### Naming Constraints
* **English Core:** The brand name **must** remain in English lettering: `DAHAB PERFUMES`. 
* **Zero Translation:** Do not translate the visual logo text, header branding, browser tabs, or footer markers into Arabic (e.g., do not write "دهب للعطور" inside the primary navigation logo or hero headers).
* **Arabic Content Exceptions:** Arabic descriptive copy, about pages, and paragraphs may use "دهب للعطور" within sentences for natural flow (e.g., "تأسس متجر دهب للعطور في قلب وسط البلد...").

---

## 2. Logo Rules & Assets
The visual identity is anchored on the metallic gold Arabic calligraphy representing "دهب" (Gold) placed over a deep black background.

### Design Integrity
* **Do Not Redesign:** The recognized calligraphy signature is the historical anchor. Keep the original lettering strokes intact.
* **Edge Cleaning:** Smooth vector paths are required to ensure the logo is sharp on ultra-retina displays.
* **Format Exports:**
  * **Gold on Black (Primary):** Gold calligraphy over dark obsidian, used for dark mode headers, loading screens, and boxes.
  * **Black on Light (Secondary):** Clean black version over warm beige background, used for light mode headers.
  * **White/Gold Minimal:** Minimal outline vector for high-end subtle print/favicon uses.

## 4. Brand Personality
DAHAB PERFUMES is represented by four key attributes:
* **Majestic & Regal:** Traditional Middle Eastern elements styled with royal elegance.
* **Artisanal & Precise:** Hand-crafted, balanced, and premium formulation.
* **Cinematic & Mystique:** Enigmatic dark lighting, shadow plays, and rich storytelling.
* **Trustworthy & Local:** Deeply rooted in Downtown Amman, offering friendly boutique service.

---

## 5. Brand Voice & Editorial Tone
Copy must sound premium, romantic, and confident. Never sound desperate or cheap.

* **Incorrect:** "خصم كبير! اشتري عطر الشعر الآن بـ 18 دينار فقط ووفّر فلوسك!" (Too cheap, like a flea market).
* **Correct:** "مجموعة معطرات الشعر الفاخرة. عبير ينساب بنعومة ليحمى خصلات شعرك مع ثبات يدوم طويلاً. متوفر الآن بـ 18.00 JOD." (Editorial, descriptive, value-driven).

---

## 6. Color Extraction & Visual Balance
The color identity uses high contrast between dark textures and metallic gold lights:
* **Midnight Backgrounds:** Represented by Obsidian Black, creating a sense of depth and mystery.
* **Glints of Gold:** Warm amber lighting reflecting off perfume bottles and gold text highlights.
* **Warm Creams:** Soft luxury galleries representing morning light in the boutique.

---

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
* Keep the main navigation header logo as `DAHAB PERFUMES`.
* Highlight product titles in Cormorant Garamond.
* Write detailed olfactory stories detailing Top, Heart, and Base notes.
* Treat the boutique's Downtown Amman heritage as a mark of local luxury.

### Don't:
* Don't translate the header text logo into "دهب للعطور".
* Don't use bright neon colors, flat gradients, or raw red/green badges.
* Don't use cheap sales banners saying "BUY NOW! SAVE 50%".
* Don't use ugly placeholder images or low-res product captures.
