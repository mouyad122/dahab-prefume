# 15. IMAGE AND PHOTOGRAPHY GUIDELINES

This document establishes the photography and image processing standards to maintain a premium visual catalog.

---

## 1. Primary Image Sourcing Strategy
* **First Priority (Official Assets):** Use official product photography from perfume houses (e.g., Lattafa, Arabian Oud) where legally appropriate.
* **Second Priority (AI Renders):** For custom lines (such as *Dahab Hair Mists* or *Eragon*), generate high-fidelity AI product renders.
* **Consistency:** Ensure all images in the catalog share a similar visual aesthetic (background textures, lighting, angles).

---

## 2. Visual Style & Aesthetic Presets (Luxury Renders)
All custom renders and product images must adhere to these guidelines:
* **Background Texture:** Use matte dark slate or black marble surfaces.
* **Backlighting:** A warm gold glow highlighting the silhouette of the bottle from behind.
* **Soft Shadows:** Soft, high-contrast shadows that ground the bottle on the surface.
* **Reflections:** A very subtle, clean reflection of the bottle on the base surface.

---

## 3. Image Specifications & Formats
* **Dimensions:** Catalog items must be square images with a **1:1** aspect ratio (minimum resolution: `800x800` pixels).
* **Format:** WebP format only to ensure fast loading times.
* **Compression:** Compress all images to keep file sizes under **80KB** for product thumbnails, and under **250KB** for large hero banners.
* **Zero Watermarks:** Never use watermarked, pixelated, or low-resolution images.

---

## 4. Hero & Category Banners
* **Editorial Feel:** Hero banners must look like high-end fragrance campaigns, featuring artistic compositions rather than simple product displays.
* **Text Contrast:** Ensure the banner image has dark overlays or sufficient contrast to keep overlay text easily readable.
